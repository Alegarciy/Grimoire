### Scrolls

The **Scrolls** chamber holds raw knowledge captured in the heat of study.
Lecture notes from the academies. Theorems scratched onto parchment mid-revelation.
Each scroll is timestamped -- a snapshot of understanding frozen at the moment
the quill touched the page.

*Current studies within:*
- **Statistical Divination** -- p-values, confidence intervals, and the eternal
  question: *does this dataset speak truth, or merely echo itself?*
- **The Overfitting Heresy** -- when a model learns the noise and mistakes the
  static for the signal. Cross-validation as the remedy; the blade that separates
  memorization from wisdom.

### Spellbook

The **Spellbook** is where theory becomes craft. These are not musings -- they are
**battle-ready incantations**, tested in the forge and sharpened for combat.

*Tomes currently bound:*

- **Vim Motions** -- *The Way of the Blade*. A comprehensive martial art for
  text manipulation. From the simple `w` step to the teleportation sigils of
  `flash.nvim`, from the `ciw` strike to the devastating regex sorcery of
  `:%s/\v`. LazyVim as the enchanted weapon of choice.

- **Databricks Bootcamp** -- *The Forge of Delta Lake*. Four weeks of deep
  immersion in the fires of data engineering: Delta Lake transformations,
  workflow orchestration, the medallion architecture of Delta Live Tables,
  and the governance wards of Unity Catalog.

## The Zettelkasten Principle

> *"A wizard is never lost. He follows the links precisely as he means to."*

This vault follows the ancient practice of **Zettelkasten** -- the "slip box" method
where each note is atomic, interconnected, and discoverable. In this Grimoire:

- **Each scroll is a single idea**, a spell component that gains power when
  linked to others
- **Links between notes form ley lines** -- pathways of meaning that let
  knowledge compound across domains
- **The graph view is the Palantir** -- revealing hidden connections between
  statistical theory, engineering practice, and tool mastery

The magic is not in any single note. It is in the **web between them**.
